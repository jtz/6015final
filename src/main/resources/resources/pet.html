About Ziye

  • Name:  Ziye
  • Color:  Gray Tuxedo
  • Breed:  Domestic Medium Hair
  • Favorite:  Salmon & Tuna

Ziye was three-month old and looked helpless when I first saw her in shelter, so I adopted her and treated her as my family. She was a troublemaker rather than a docile pet when she was young. When grown up, she became a warm sensible girl, and she always shared her favorite toys with me.
